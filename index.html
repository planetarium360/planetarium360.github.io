Intro

Planetarium360 offers a solution to the existing challenges in space science education.

Innovation

First made-in Indonesia multiplatform planetarium app developed by junior high schoolers with Bahasa content.

Accessibility

Planetarium360 can run on any available devices and platforms without requiring internet connection.

Cost Saving

Students can now access digital planetarium for free without needing to travel.

Reducing Carbon Footprint

Eliminating the need to travel to a planetarium can reduce gas emissions.

Transforming Space Education

15M+ Potential user base

Solution

Planetarium360 is designed to run in multiple platforms making it accessible on a wide range of devices used by Indonesian students.

Innovative Solution

We've developed high quality app to address the critical issue faced by Indonesia. Interactive and immersive app can help to address the declining interest in STEM among Indonesian youth.

Business Model

We strive to become a social startup developing educational apps to support space education. We are inviting space industry stakeholders to become our partners in this project.

Development Plan

The Planetarium360 app has been fully developed and is ready for use. We welcome feedback from sponsors to help improving the app with new features.

Step 01

Biweekly Demo

Conducting biweekly or monthly meeting with our stakeholders to showcase progrees and gather input to enhance the app.

Step 02

Development Commitment

Dedicating 2 hours/day as the part of digital curriculum in our school.

Step 03

Milestone & Delivery

Releasing updates or new apps every two quarters.

Step 04

Marketing

Promoting the app to Indonesian students via webiste and social media ads. To promote Planetarium360, we plan to develop an official website and social media channels supported by targeted advertising
